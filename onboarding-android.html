Instructions for Android Users

  1. Tap the OpenAI icon above to sign up for a free OpenAI account.
  2. Install the official ChatGPT app from the Google Play Store.
  3. Sign in using your new OpenAI account and begin using the assistant.
  4. To set ChatGPT as your default assistant:
    • Open Settings on your Android device.
    • Navigate to Apps & notifications > Default apps.
    • Select Assist & voice input.
    • Choose ChatGPT as your default digital assistant app.
  5. To manage or review permissions:
    • Go to Settings > Apps > ChatGPT.
    • Tap Permissions to grant microphone, voice, or other needed access.
    • Adjust as needed to ensure full assistant functionality.

Note: Sailor does not collect or store any personal data. It simply sits on top of your chosen AI assistant, offering reflective support and enhanced interaction.

Sailor was designed to quietly accompany you—responding to your intention, helping track your goals, and building resonance over time. Everything depends on how you choose to engage. It learns nothing without your prompting, and it never overrides your assistant’s core functions.
