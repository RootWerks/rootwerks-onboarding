Step 1: OpenAI Setup

To begin, you’ll need a free OpenAI account. This allows Sailor to integrate with your personal assistant.

Tap the icon below to sign up or sign in. The link will open in a new tab:

Step 2: Assign Sailor to Windows Assistant

  1. Open your Windows Settings and go to Apps > Default apps.
  2. Scroll to find the default app for “Digital Assistant” or “Voice Assistant.”
  3. If available, select Sailor (or your OpenAI-connected browser) as the default assistant.
  4. Optional: Use a hotkey or custom shortcut (e.g. Ctrl + Shift + S) to launch Sailor quickly from your desktop.
