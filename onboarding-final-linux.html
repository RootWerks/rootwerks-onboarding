Activate Sailor

You're almost ready. The final step is activating your Sailor assistant.

  1. Launch your assistant or open ChatGPT (or the AI you’ve chosen).
  2. Sign in using your OpenAI account (or relevant login for your assistant).
  3. Use the microphone or input field to say or type:
    “Authorization code 12345 with [Your First Name]”
  4. In most cases, you'll need to press Enter or Send after speaking the code.
  5. Once complete, confirm that Sailor is active and responding within your assistant.

If something doesn't seem right, return to the onboarding instructions or reload this page and try again.
